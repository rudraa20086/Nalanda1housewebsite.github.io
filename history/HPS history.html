HPS History

The hostory of The Hyderabad Public School

1919

In 1919, a proposal was presented to the Seventh Nizam, H.E.H. Mir Osman Ali Khan by the Hon’ble H. Weikfield, Director General of the Court of Wards (Department of Revenue) to establish a fully residential school on the lines of Eton College, London, to give the sons of Jagirdars an enriching learning experience.

1923

In 1923, the Jagirdar’s College opened its doors to five students and six teachers under the first Founder-Principal Mr.H.W.Shawcross.

1929

The first batch appeared for Senior Cambridge ‘O’ levels in 1929 just 6 years after the opening of the school in 1923.

1930

By 1930, the strength of the School had risen to 150. The bountiful land for the school was bequeathed by various Jagirdars and purchased from generous civilians. H.E. Lady Vicar-Ul-Umra gave 89 acres of land from her Begumpet Estate.

1950

In 1950, the official HPS societ was formed, consosting of officials, nizams and generous land donors. They as the shareholders of The Hyderabad Public School.

1973

In 1973, The Hyderabad Public School celebrateed it's 50th anniversary Golden Jubilee celebrations. The celebrations included students performing various performances like dancing, singing, yoga, and other sports.

1998

In 1998, The Hyderabad Public School was officially 75 years old. The ocassion was celebrated with a multi-day celebration event. These celebrations included famous personalities coming to school, dances, performance arts, vintage car meets, etc.

2013

In 2013, The Hyderabad Public School celebrated it's 90th year anniversary in a magnificient fashion with a multi-day event. The celebrations started with a vintage car drive and showcase after that there was a school concert where acts of performing arts were performed, Eagle carnival filled with student workshops carnival rides and more, the event finally ended with a musical concert performed by famous singer 'Adnan Sami'.

2023

In 2023, The Hyderabad Public School is planning to celebrate it 100th anniversary. The celebrations will pay tribute to thee original founders of the school and the school's rich culture and history. We are welcoming you to join us in our celebration.
